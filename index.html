About ME

I was born in Berlin, Germany, to missionaries Michael and Marsha Talley. I spent 20 years serving there with my parents before coming to study Criminal Justice at Bob Jones University. As of the coming fall, I will officially be considered a Sophomore. 

Much of my time is spent working for either Public Safety or the BJU Post Office. I also serve as the Athletic Director for my society, the Chi Epsilon Delta Wolves. On Sundays I attend Faith Baptist Church in Easley SC, where I sing in the church choir. 

My extracurriculars include writing for Inkwell Magazine, singing in the Bob Jones University Concert Choir, and helping my parents with their ministry back home.
